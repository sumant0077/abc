S.N.J.P.S.N.M.S. TRUST'S DEGREE COLLEGE - NIDASOSHI

The BCA institution established in the year 2007 with the purpose of providing quality technical education. Since then the growth of the college has been guided by the proactive response to the fast changing world of technology. BSc and BCom streams are established in the year 2022. S.N.J.P.S.N.M.S. TRUST'S DEGREE COLLEGE - Nidasoshi, has emerged as the best institute in this region, most popular among the student community seeking professional education. The institute is affiliated to Rani Channamma University, Belagavi (RCU, Belagavi). The institute is also recognized by Government of Karnataka. The primary focus of the college is to establish premier educational institutions in the fields of science and technology, thereby enabling the students to emerge as conscious, dynamic and responsible citizen of the nation. It also helps students to achieve success in their life by serving the students procure good technical education to get recruited into various multinational companies.
